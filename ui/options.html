About

QuietJunk

No noise. No clutter.

QuietJunk is built to quietly remove false unread pressure by clearing junk mail counts behind the scenes. It stays passive by default and gives just enough control for people with multiple accounts or unusual folder behavior.

Current Capabilities

  • Watches new mail events after Thunderbird spam classification.
  • Clears unread status in junk folders across accounts.
  • Supports startup cleanup with configurable delay.
  • Lets you exclude specific accounts from automation.

Testing Notes

  • Load this add-on temporarily from `manifest.json`.
  • Use Debug logging when validating real-world junk behavior.
