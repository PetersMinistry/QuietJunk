About

QuietJunk

Less noise. More calm.

QuietJunk keeps unread spam from piling up in the background. It stays out of your way, cleans up the junk, and gives you a few helpful controls when you want them.

Version 0.1.0
Manifest MV3

Current Capabilities

  • Watches new mail after Thunderbird sorts out the spam.
  • Clears unread junk across your accounts.
  • Can do a startup cleanup after a delay you control.
  • Lets you leave specific accounts alone.

Testing Notes

  • Load this add-on temporarily from `manifest.json`.
  • Turn on Debug logging if you want the play-by-play.
  • Check the counter after startup cleanup or fresh junk mail.
